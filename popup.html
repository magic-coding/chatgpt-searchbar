ChatGPT Searchbar Extension

The ChatGPT Searchbar Extension is a convenient and quick front-end solution to add a search feature to the ChatGPT website. This extension makes it easier to search for specific conversations on the ChatGPT website until the OpenAI team implements this feature natively.

After installing the extension, you can use the search input that appears at the top of the chat window to filter your conversations based on the text inside them. It's a simple yet effective way to locate the conversation you're looking for without having to scroll through your entire chat history.
